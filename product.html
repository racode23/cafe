Cofee Latte

Our classic latte is the perfect harmony of rich, smooth espresso and creamy steamed milk. Crafted with a double shot of freshly brewed espresso, it’s gently blended with velvety steamed milk and topped with a thin layer of silky microfoam. The result is a mellow, comforting coffee experience that’s both satisfying and elegant. Whether you enjoy it plain or with a dash of cinnamon or flavored syrup, the latte is a timeless favorite for those who love a balanced, creamy coffee without overpowering bitterness. Served hot or iced, it’s an everyday indulgence that energizes and soothes in equal measure.
